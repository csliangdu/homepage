    _I am a Ph. D student in Institute of Software, Chinese Academy of Sciences. I am supervised by Prof. Angsheng Li. Before that, I obtained my Bachelor degree of Mathematics from Beijing Normal University in 2007.

    I am on the job market. If you have any questions, please feel free to email me.

    Research Interests:

    _My research interests lie on the intersection between theoretical computer science and network science. Currently, I am focused on random graph models and local algorithmic paradigms for handing large scale network data, including property testing, streaming and distributed algorithms, local exploration algorithms.
